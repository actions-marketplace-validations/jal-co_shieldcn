Public badges are free forever — that part never changes. Paid plans add
identity, control, and insight around your badges, and keep shieldcn
sustainable.

There are two paid tiers:

- **Plus — $8/mo**, for individual maintainers who live in their READMEs.
- **Pro — $30/mo**, for companies managing a brand across many repos.

## The core Pro promise

**Change your brand once, every distributed asset updates.** A Pro *brand* is a
named set of style tokens plus hosted logos and fonts. Reference it by URL from
your READMEs, npm pages, and docs sites. Edit the brand in the dashboard and
every embed that references it re-renders on the next fetch — no PRs across 40
repos when the company rebrands.

```md
![build](https://shieldcn.dev/github/acme/app/ci.svg?brand=acme)
```

Change `acme`'s color, logo, or font in the dashboard, and that badge — and
every other `?brand=acme` embed anywhere — follows.

## How billing works

Billing runs through [Polar](https://polar.sh) as the merchant of record (it
handles global tax). Checkout, upgrades (Plus → Pro), and cancellation all
happen in the Polar customer portal, linked from your
[dashboard](/dashboard).

Plans are org-scoped: a subscription entitles an **organization** (your company
or team), and every saved README, brand, and analytics view belongs to that
org. Create or switch orgs from the account menu.

<InlineHint variant="note">Grace handling: if a subscription lapses, your brands and saved READMEs go **read-only but keep serving** — shipped badges never break, and you can still open and export everything. Editing resumes when you re-subscribe.</InlineHint>
