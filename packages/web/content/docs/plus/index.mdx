Public badges are free forever — that part never changes. Plus is mainly a way
to support the project and keep it independent; it happens to add identity and
control around your badges as a thank-you.

There is one paid tier:

- **Plus — $10/mo**, support shieldcn and get a few perks for maintainers who
  live in their READMEs.

## The core Plus promise

**Change your brand once, every distributed asset updates.** A Plus *brand* is a
named set of style tokens plus hosted logos and fonts. Reference it by URL from
your READMEs, npm pages, and docs sites. Edit the brand in the dashboard and
every embed that references it re-renders on the next fetch — no PRs across 40
repos when you rebrand.

```md
![build](https://shieldcn.dev/github/acme/app/ci.svg?brand=acme)
```

Change `acme`'s color, logo, or font in the dashboard, and that badge — and
every other `?brand=acme` embed anywhere — follows.

## How billing works

Billing runs through [Polar](https://polar.sh) as the merchant of record (it
handles global tax). Checkout and cancellation happen in the Polar customer
portal, linked from your [dashboard](/dashboard).

Your subscription entitles your personal account, and every saved README and
brand belongs to it.

<InlineHint variant="note">Grace handling: if a subscription lapses, your brand and saved READMEs go **read-only but keep serving** — shipped badges never break, and you can still open and export everything. Editing resumes when you re-subscribe.</InlineHint>
